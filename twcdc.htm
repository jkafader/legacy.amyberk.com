Together We Can Defeat Capitalism

Together We Can Defeat Capitalism (TWCDC) hosted gratis low tea at e://mission art space in San Francisco for one month in 2000. This community art event inluded a live web cam, guerrilla wallpaper, fine china and a furry rabbit hole entrance to the socialist utopia.

Together We Can Defeat Capitalism (TWCDC) conducted spectacular traffic-stopping actions throughout San Francisco to advertise the shortfalls of the capitalist system. The actions took place on MAY 1, 2000, the day on which socialism is traditionally celebrated around the globe.
